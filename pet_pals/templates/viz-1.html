This visual explores discrepancy between price paid to growers and the quality of the coffee. To do this we found the difference between price paid to grower and global retail price average. The metric "Price to Grower Disparity" is an attempt to combine retail price,price to grower, and average quality score. The higher the score, the greater the share of revenue the grower's saw. Price to Grower Disparity is defined as: Avg. Quality Score/(Avg.Retail-(Avg.Price to Grower))
